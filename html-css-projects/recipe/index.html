Creamy Garlic Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 2 cloves Garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley(optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water al dente
  2. Saute garlic in olive oil for 1-2 minutes
  3. Add cream and stir over low heat
  4. Drain pasta and mix into sauce
  5. Season with salt, pepper and persley. Serve hot!
